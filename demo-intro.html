Proving that intent-based invocation is probabilistic,
and command-driven invocation is deterministic.
Command Mode
Deterministic. The control plane enforces every workflow step in fixed order. Missing a required section = hard failure. The audit ledger guarantees requiredSteps === executedSteps.
Intent Mode
Probabilistic. A natural language router interprets what to run. Steps may be silently skipped. The output looks fine—but the ledger reveals the gap between what should run and what did run.
